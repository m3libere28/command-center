Why Beckham Law matters for you

Under the Beckham regime, your US-source income (VA disability + dividends) is treated as foreign income and is generally exempt from Spanish taxation for up to 6 years. Emily's Kickoff contractor income, if structured as foreign-source, may also qualify. Filing Modelo 149 promptly after Social Security registration is the single highest-leverage tax action for this move. Consult a Spanish gestor who specializes in the DNV/Beckham combination before your first Spanish tax year closes.
